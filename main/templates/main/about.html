It all started with a simple idea:

The idea that people facing the court system should be able to know what they're up against, without paying a lawyer first. What if we could show people what happens in cases like theirs by analyzing the thousands of cases like theirs that have come before and providing that information in an easy-to-understand format?

How it works:

Data is power. Our system collects data from each jurisdiction and analyzes it based on case type, judge, whether or not the person in that case hired an attorney, and what happened in that case. With that data you can now see what the most common outcome for your case is, and whether or not hiring an attorney will give you an advantage.

Taking the guesswork out of hiring an attorney:

Our system identifies the attorneys with the highest success rate for each type of case before each judge, so you know which attorney will be best able to handle your case. Our proprietary formula evaluates each attorney based on a number of factors so that you can make an educated decision about who to hire.
